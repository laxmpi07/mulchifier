Transforming food waste into nutrient-rich mulch for a greener future.

Our Process

1. Collection

Gather food scraps from school cafeterias.

2. Mixing

Shred food scraps into smaller pieces.

3. Decomposition

Allow the material to break down.

4. Implementation

Apply the finished mulch to gardens.
